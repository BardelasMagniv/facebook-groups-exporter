# Privacy Policy for Facebook Groups Exporter

**Last updated: December 3, 2025**

## Overview

Facebook Groups Exporter ("the Extension") is a browser extension developed by Kfir Ben Porat. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles user data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The Extension operates entirely on your local device. Here's what you should know:

### What the Extension Accesses
- The Extension only accesses Facebook.com pages when you are on the Facebook Groups page
- It reads group names and links from the page you are currently viewing
- This data is only processed locally in your browser

### What the Extension Does NOT Do
- ❌ Does not collect personal information
- ❌ Does not track your browsing activity
- ❌ Does not store any data on external servers
- ❌ Does not transmit data to third parties
- ❌ Does not use cookies or tracking technologies
- ❌ Does not access your Facebook account credentials

## Data Storage

All exported data (JSON files) is saved directly to your local device through your browser's download functionality. No data is stored by the Extension itself or transmitted to any external servers.

## Permissions Used

The Extension requires the following permissions:

- **activeTab**: To access the current Facebook tab when you click the Export button
- **tabs**: To check if you're on the Facebook Groups page and navigate there if needed
- **scripting**: To run the content script that reads group information from the page
- **Host permission (facebook.com)**: To operate on Facebook's website

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of exporting your Facebook groups list.

## Third-Party Services

This Extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Children's Privacy

This Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.
